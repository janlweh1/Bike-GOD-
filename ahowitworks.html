Simple. Fast. Reliable.

Renting a bike has never been easier. Follow these simple steps to start your journey.

Browse & Select

Explore our wide range of bikes through our website or mobile app. Filter by type, price, and location to find the perfect bike for your needs. Each bike listing shows real-time availability.

2

Verify & Pay

Create your account or log in if you already have one. Complete a quick identity verification process for security purposes. This only needs to be done once and takes less than 2 minutes.

Choose your rental duration (hourly or daily) and proceed to secure payment. We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Your payment information is encrypted and secure.

Unlock & Ride

Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a unique unlock code and GPS directions to your bike. Navigate to the bike's location using our built-in map feature.

Simply scan the QR code on the bike or enter your unlock code to release the lock. Adjust the seat height, check the brakes, and you're ready to ride! Your rental timer starts automatically when you unlock the bike.

Return & Review

When you're done, return the bike to any designated parking zone shown on the app. Make sure the bike is properly locked and parked in an authorized area to avoid additional fees.

Take a photo of the parked bike through the app to confirm return. Your rental automatically ends, and you'll receive a receipt via email. Share your experience by leaving a review to help other riders!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting bikes

Do I need a license to rent a bike?

No license is required for regular bikes. For electric bikes, you must be 18+ and have a valid ID for verification purposes.

What if the bike gets damaged?

Report any damage immediately through the app. Minor wear and tear is covered, but significant damage may incur repair fees as per our policy.

Can I extend my rental time?

Yes! You can extend your rental anytime through the app before your current rental period ends. Additional charges will apply based on the extended duration.

What happens if I return late?

Late returns incur additional charges at 1.5x the hourly rate. Repeated late returns may affect your account standing. Please return on time or extend your rental.
